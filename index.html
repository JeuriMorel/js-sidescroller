    • how to play

  • movement

    • Roll: Press right or left while crouching, or Quarter Circle right/ left.

    • Float: Hold up while falling.

  • attacks

    • Scratch: Press action while standing. (can be cancelled into a roll by pressing left or right on hit)

    • Up roll: Press action while rolling, or Press action while jumping.

    • down roll: Press action while falling.

    • Dash: Press action while running at top speed. (Can be held for a damage boost. Can be cancelled with roll during early frames by pressing left)

  • misc

    • Taunt: Hold action while crouching. (Temporarily lower susceptible enemies' defence)

    • Pause: double tap screen
